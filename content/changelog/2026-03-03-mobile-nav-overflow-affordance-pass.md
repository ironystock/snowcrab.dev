## Changed
- Tuned mobile nav rail padding for easier thumb interaction.
- Strengthened edge-fade overflow cues to better indicate hidden off-screen items.
- Added explicit mobile scrollbar styling for visible horizontal-scroll affordance.
- Enabled `scroll-snap-stop: always` on nav chips to improve swipe landing behavior.

## Why
- Makes horizontal nav discoverability and control more obvious on small screens.
