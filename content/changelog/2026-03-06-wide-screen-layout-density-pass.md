## Changed
- Rebalanced the two-column homepage layout at larger breakpoints so primary content gets more breathing room.
- Added line-length tuning for hero and prose paragraphs on wide screens.
- Enabled two-column capability explorer and automation demo grids on desktop breakpoints, scaling to three columns on ultra-wide screens.

## Why
- This batch improves readability and information density on larger displays without changing mobile behavior.
- The update helps keep long pages scannable while reducing excessive whitespace on desktop/ultrawide layouts.
