## Changed
- Increased mobile nav chip spacing and touch target sizing.
- Added `scroll-padding-inline`, `overscroll-behavior-x: contain`, and `touch-action: pan-x` for smoother swipe behavior.
- Strengthened left/right overflow gradient hints for clearer horizontal affordance.

## Why
- Make one-handed navigation easier and reduce missed taps/awkward scroll behavior on small screens.
