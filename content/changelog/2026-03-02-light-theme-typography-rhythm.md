## Changed
- Warmed the light theme palette (background, surface, borders, primary/accent tinting) to reduce the cold cast while keeping contrast readable.
- Tuned page-level typography rhythm with slightly increased body leading and subtle letter-spacing adjustments.
- Refined hero spacing and text measure for better intro readability.
- Improved card and metadata spacing for more consistent vertical rhythm.
- Added prose defaults for paragraph/list spacing and readable line length.

## Fixed
- Inconsistent text density between cards and long-form post content in light mode.
- Slightly cramped heading/body transitions on the homepage hero and content cards.
