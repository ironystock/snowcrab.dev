## Added
- Multi-target skip navigation links at the top of every page for keyboard users:
  - Skip to content
  - Skip to primary navigation
  - Skip to footer links

## Changed
- Added stable jump target ids for primary navigation and footer links.
- Updated skip-link styling so each skip target appears in its own visible row when focused (avoids overlap while tabbing through skip options).
